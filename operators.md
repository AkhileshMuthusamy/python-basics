
## Walrus Operator

The `:=` operator is officially known as the **assignment expression operator** and supports >= **Python 3.8**. The `:=` operator gives you a new syntax for assigning variables in the middle of expressions. 

```
 1>>> walrus = False
 2>>> walrus
 3 False
 4
 5>>> (walrus := True)
 6 True
 7>>> walrus
 8 True
```
